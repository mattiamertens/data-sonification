Bio
Born in 2002, Alessio began studying piano at the age of 5 and composition at 11. By the age of 22, he had completed a Master’s in Piano, a Bachelor’s in Composition, a Second-Level Master’s in Piano, a Master’s in Film Composition, all with honors. He is currently attending a second Master’s in Piano.
